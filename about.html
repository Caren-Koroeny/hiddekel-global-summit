Priters Expo Summit 2023

PRINTING United Expo is a strategic response to meet the industry’s need for a one-roof approach. This approach delivers the strongest ROI for printers (attendees) by providing one place to access new applications, technology, and products. In return this produces the strongest ROI for exhibitors by delivering every printing segment in one location. Hiddekel Printing Expo is an opportunity to support your current customers and produce net new opportunities

It has been toooooo long since we have been able to attend an industry tradeshow. PRINTING United Expo is a ‘never miss’ event for our team because it’s the best way to keep up with the technology and trends. Keeping up with the latest innovations is paramount for us. These last couple of years without a show have been the longest stretch without upgrading hardware, software, or seeing new materials available. It will be time to spend money. Besides seeing new technology changes, I miss my friends! Relationships and networking with industry colleagues have become invaluable assets over the years.
